OUR STORY

What is Shameless Product Placement?

Shameless Product Placement is the newest, most revolutionary step in advertisement! We work with advertisers and allow them to place virtual advertisements fully integrated within our game, completely eliminating the ideology that advertisements are "to be skipped" if they are a core part of the game! We work with game developers who want to add a source of revenue into their game, by adding virtual advertising to it!
