OUR STORY

What is Shameless Product Placement?

Video games are stepping up to fill the entertainment void left by uninspired traditional TV programming and repetitive live sports that no longer captivate fans as they once did. People of all ages are seeking more interactive and engaging entertainment experiences. They're tired of simply watching—they want to be part of the story! They want to "be the ball"! They crave alternative storylines, lovable characters, social interactions, and competitive gameplay. Our adventure is just beginning. Join us— the best is yet to come!
